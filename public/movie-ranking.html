The Top Marvel Movies According to Omar

My top 3 Marvel movies of all time.

  1. Captain America: The Winter Soldier

    • This is my all-time favorite Marvel movie. The spy thriller vibe makes it engaging, offering a really thrilling watch.

  2. Avengers: Infinity War

    • The first truly epic gathering of all my favorite superheroes.

  3. Thor: Ragnarok

    • By far the best space adventure and the best Thor film.
